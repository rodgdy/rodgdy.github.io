What are Senties?

Senties are unique and cute collectible NFTs, programmatically generated from different traits on the Ethereum blockchain. The collection consists of 1,000 randomly assembled cool creatures from over 2 million+ possible combinations.

There are nearly endless combinations -- Accessories, Colors, Hand Accessories like Diamond Hands, Swords, and themed Backgrounds for its own unique look and so many possibilities!

Specs

Each Sentie is a programmatically generated 48x48 GIF enlarged to 480x480, stored as a token on the Ethereum blockchain. Each one is unique, carefully crafted from more than 6 properties, and animated with 5 different frames combined and generated by code.

How rare my Senties is?

Traits are where the rarity is determined.

Listed traits are: Accessory, Body, Color, Hand and Stage
Unlisted trait are: Foreground effects **

Traits and rarity can be measured in percentages:
Cool traits range from 4% to 30%
Rare traits are 1% of the collection
Super Rare traits are 0,5% of the collection
Unique traits are only 0,1% of the collection and the most rare

** Foreground effects such as speech bubbles, sparks and effects are not listed as traits by design so that you need to look at the effect to spot the differences.
