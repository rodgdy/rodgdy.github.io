What are Senties?

Senties are unique and cute collectible NFTs, programmatically generated from a combination of various different traits on the Ethereum blockchain. The collection consists of 1,000 randomly assembled cool creatures from over 2 million+ possible combinations.

There are so many possibilities with traits like Accessories, Colors, Hand Accessories (Diamond Hands, Swords, Snails, etc) and themed Backgrounds giving each Senties their own unique look!

Specs

Each Sentie is a programmatically generated 48x48 GIF enlarged to 480x480 and stored as an ERC-1155 token on the Ethereum blockchain. Each one is unique, carefully crafted from more than 6 properties, and animated with 5 different frames combined and generated by code.

How rare my Senties is?

Rarity is determined using traits

Listed traits are: Accessory, Body, Color, Hand and Stage
Unlisted trait are: Foreground effects **

Traits and rarity can be measured in percentages:
Cool traits are within a range of 4% to 30%
Rare traits represents 1% of the collection
Super Rare traits represents 0,5% of the collection
Unique traits represents only 0,1% of the collection and are the rarest

** By design, Foreground effects such as speech bubbles, sparks, and effects were not listed as traits so that collectors need to look at the effect to spot the differences.

FAQ

  • I am buying a NFT for the first time, how can I get started?
    Install a Metamask chrome extension (https://metamask.io/). Charge it with ETH through services that allow you to change money to ETH (Coinbase, Paypal etc..). Once your wallet is setup and loaded with ETH, you should be good to go! Browse to the collection page on the Opensea website: https://opensea.io/collections/senties to see available Senties.
    Sign in your Metamask wallet chrome extension than select the one you’d like to adopt, click the Buy and approve the transaction with the Metamask plugin. Congratulations!!! You are now a Senties owner!
